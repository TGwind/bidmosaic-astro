1. What is Bidmosaic H5 Interactive Advertising?

H5 Interactive Advertising is a lightweight interactive ad format built on HTML5 technology, allowing users to participate directly without downloading. Compared to traditional creatives, its core advantage lies in "participatable, immersive, and convertible."

1.1 Interactive-Driven Creative Expression

Bidmosaic H5 supports various interactive formats, such as:

  • Slide-to-unlock experiences
  • Quiz/assessment interactions
  • Simulated operation trials
  • Prize wheels and mini-games
  • Multi-step storyline guidance

This interactive mechanism effectively extends user dwell time, enhances ad memorability, and naturally embeds product selling points during the interaction process.

1.2 Programmatic Capabilities

Bidmosaic H5 is not merely a creative display tool but deeply integrated with the programmatic advertising system to achieve:

  • Precise audience targeting
  • Real-time bidding
  • Multi-dimensional data tracking
  • Conversion path optimization

Creative and placement strategies form a closed loop, truly enabling "interaction equals conversion."
